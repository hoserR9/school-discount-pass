Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Del Norte Nighthawk Discount Card Program ("Program," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information associated with the Discount Card.

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase, claim, or use the Card, we may collect:

  • Cardholder Name — provided voluntarily when claiming the digital card. You may use the Card without providing your name.
  • Scan Data — when your Card QR code is scanned at a participating business, we record the date, time, and business name.
  • Device Information — when you add the Card to Apple Wallet, Apple provides a device identifier and push notification token to enable Card updates. We do not access your personal Apple ID, contacts, location, or other device data.
  • IP Address and Browser Information — collected automatically when you access our web pages. Used for security and fraud prevention only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Card Verification — to confirm your Card is valid when scanned at a business.
  • Fraud Prevention — to detect unauthorized sharing or misuse of Cards.
  • Push Notifications — to send Card updates including new promotions, changed business listings, and program announcements via Apple Wallet.
  • Aggregate Reporting — to provide participating businesses with anonymized usage statistics (total visits, unique visitor counts, peak times). Individual cardholder identities are not shared with businesses.
  • Program Improvement — to understand Card usage patterns and improve the program for future seasons.

3. Information Shared with Sponsors

Participating businesses ("Sponsors") receive access to a dashboard showing:

  • Total number of Card scans at their location
  • Number of unique Card IDs (not names) that visited
  • Scan dates and times
  • Card IDs associated with scans (anonymized identifiers only)

We do not share cardholder names, contact information, or device data with Sponsors.

4. Data Retention

  • Scan data and Card records are retained for the duration of the Card's validity period plus 12 months for reporting purposes.
  • Device registration tokens are deleted when a cardholder removes the Card from Apple Wallet or when the Card expires.
  • After the retention period, data is permanently deleted.

5. Data Security

  • All Card authentication tokens are uniquely generated and cryptographically secure.
  • Communication between Apple Wallet and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS.
  • Database access is restricted to Program administrators.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

The Card may be used by minors (high school students). We minimize data collection — the only personal information collected is an optional name. We do not knowingly collect email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal information from minors through the Card program. Parents or guardians may contact us to request deletion of their child's data.

7. Your Rights

  • You may request a copy of data associated with your Card ID.
  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting the Program.
  • You may remove the Card from Apple Wallet at any time, which stops push notifications and removes device registration.
  • California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Card constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or data requests, contact the Del Norte High School Football program office.
