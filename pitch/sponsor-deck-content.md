### Slide 2: The Opportunity
- Del Norte Football has a passionate community of students, parents, alumni, and fans
- Every cardholder carries your brand in their Apple Wallet — visible every time they open their phone
- Unlike a physical card that sits in a drawer, a digital card sends notifications and stays top-of-mind

---

### Slide 3: How It Works — For the Customer
1. Fan visits our page or scans a link and taps "Add to Apple Wallet"
2. The Nighthawk Discount Card appears in their Wallet — gold and navy, school branding
3. At your business, they show the card or the cashier scans the QR code
4. Discount is applied — simple, fast, no paper coupons

---

### Slide 4: How It Works — For the Sponsor
- Your business name and discount are listed on the back of every digital card
- When a customer scans the QR code at your location, that visit is tracked
- You receive reports showing how many visits and unique customers came through the program
- No POS changes needed — cashier just scans and applies the discount like a regular coupon

---

### Slide 5: Why Digital Beats Physical
| Physical Card | Digital Card |
|---|---|
| Gets lost, forgotten at home | Always in their phone |
| No way to track usage | Every scan is tracked |
| Static — can't change once printed | Push new deals anytime |
| One-time impression | Ongoing engagement via notifications |
| Printing costs | Zero printing costs |

- We can push limited-time promotions directly to every cardholder's phone
- Examples:
  - "Game Day Special: 20% off at Board & Brew — tonight only!"
  - "Homecoming Weekend: Buy one get one at Baskin Robbins"
  - "End of Season: Double discount at all sponsors this week"
- Cardholders get a lock-screen notification and their card updates automatically
- This drives real foot traffic to your business on the days that matter most
